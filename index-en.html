Medical Device Regulatory Catalogue Ontology: Description back to ToC

This ontology models the European catalogue of high-risk medical devices and in vitro diagnostic devices under the regulatory framework of the MDR (EU 2017/745) and IVDR (EU 2017/746) regulations.
